Who We Are

208PcService is a computer repair and IT support service focused on helping people fix, improve, and maintain their desktop and laptop computers. We specialize in fast, affordable, and reliable solutions for everyday PC problems. We provide professional computer services including virus removal, system diagnostics, hardware installation, Windows troubleshooting, OS installation, and performance tuning. We ONLY work with computers — no phones, no consoles, no data recovery services. Our goal is to make computer repair simple and accessible. We aim to deliver honest pricing, clear communication, and quality service every time.
